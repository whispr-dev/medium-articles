1. What is a US Address? (From index.html blurbs)[file:2]
An address is a location pointer: house/street number, city, ZIP/postcode, state, country.[file:2] It IDs buildings/properties in organized areas for mail routing/delivery via special codes like ZIP.[file:2]

Uses:
- Locate buildings/geos.[file:2]
- ID structures/properties.[file:2]
- Postal mail sorting.[file:2]
- Census/stats collection.[file:2]
- Town planning/resource allocation.[file:2]

2. Standard US Mailing Format (index.html & house addr pages)[file:2][file:3]
Recipient Name (full, w/ middle init: Samuel L. Thompson)[file:2]
Street: 1026 Oakmound Road (house # + street; add Apt/Suite if multi-unit)[file:2][file:3]
City: Chicago[file:2]
State: Illinois - IL (full or 2-letter abbr, uppercase no punct)[file:2]
ZIP: 60605 (5-digit) or 60605-1234 (ZIP+4)[file:2]
Country: USA (intl only)[file:2]

Full example: 1026 Oakmound Road, Chicago, Illinois 60605 USA[file:2]

House/Street Addresses (ushouseaddress.html): Numeric position on street + name (e.g., 8619 S Wolcott Ave).[file:3] Directions (N/S/E/W) clarify duplicates; suffixes std: St=Street, Ave=Avenue.[file:2]

3. US vs UK Formats (index.html w/ img file:9)[file:2][file:9]
US: Name, Street #/Name, City, State ZIP, USA.[file:2][file:9]
UK: Name, Street/Number, Locality (e.g., Clachan of Glendaruel), Postcode (PA22 7QH alphanumeric), UK.[file:2][file:9]
PO Box US: GlobalSet Express LLC, PO Box 1230, Chicago, Illinois 60605 USA[file:2][file:9]
PO Box UK: Kennedy Productions, PO Box 4235, Clachan of Glendaruel PA22 7QH UK[file:2][file:9]

Intl: Research per country; UK postcodes mix nums/letters; some (Romania) use house # no ZIP.[file:2]

4. Postal vs Residential (index.html)[file:2]
Postal: PO Box/business for mail-only, hides street (private).[file:2]
Residential: Street/home for mail + visitors.[file:2] ~161.4M total US addresses (2021 USPS).[file:2]

5. PO Boxes Deep Dive (poboxaddresses.html & index.html)[file:5][file:2]
PO Box: Secure post office locker – "PO Box ####, City ST ZIP" (no street).[file:5][file:2]
How it works: Mail routes direct to PO; pick up w/ key/code.[file:5]
Get one: USPS/Royal Mail equiv; pay-as-you-go or virtual office bundles.[file:5]
Why: Privacy (hide home), multi-market presence, travel forwarding, faster delivery (sorting office).[file:5]

6. ZIP Code Structure (uspostalzipcodegenerator.html & index.html)[file:8][file:2]
5-digit (1963 ZIP plan):
- Digit 1: Region (0=NE, 9=West/AK/HI)[file:2]
- 2-3: Sectional Center Facility (regional hub)[file:2]
- 4-5: Local post office/delivery zone[file:2]
ZIP+4 (1983): -ABCD (A-B=sector streets/block; C-D=segment side/floor)[file:2]

Examples: 90210 (9=West, 02=LA hub, 10=Beverly Hills local).[file:2] Tools verify/add ZIPs.[file:8]

Postal vs ZIP: ZIP=US-specific; postal codes global equivs.[file:8]

7. US Phone Numbers (Across generators: index, phones.html, random-phones.html)[file:2][file:4][file:6]
Format: (Area) XXX-XXX-XXXX or XXX XXX-XXXX (10 digits).[file:2]
- Area Code: 3 digits, geo (773=Chicago, 212=NYC).[file:2][file:4]
- Exchange: 3 digits (local switch).[file:4]
- Line: 4 digits.[file:6]
Fakes like 773-238-7162 pair w/ addresses for realism.[file:2]

8. History & Evolution (index.html deep sections)[file:2]
- 1775: First post office (Philly).[file:2]
- 1789: Post Office Dept.[file:2]
- 1792: Addr rules (name, town/county/state).[file:2]
- 1800s: House #s in cities; zones in big towns.[file:2]
- 1847: Stamps; 1863: City delivery; 1896: Rural Free.[file:2]
- 1963: ZIP; 1983: ZIP+4; 1990s: DPV validation.[file:2]
USPS stds suffixes/abbrevs; AMS/CASS for accuracy.[file:2]

9. Why Generators/Fake Addrs? (All pages)[file:2][file:3][file:5][file:8]
Privacy (no real addr on shady sites), testing forms, research, cyber sec (public PCs).[file:2] Select state → shuffle real-ish streets/cities/ZIPs/phones.[file:2]
