## Part 4: The Spicy Stuff (Riot Control)
### It’s Not Just "Gas," It’s a Powder Party

You’ve seen it on the news. Tear gas. Pepper spray. The "non-lethal" stuff. But what is it, actually?

### CS Gas (Tear Gas)
Spoiler Alert: It’s not a gas. It’s a white solid powder (2-chlorobenzalmalononitrile) that they light on fire to create smoke.

How it Works: It hijacks a specific receptor in your body called TRPA1. This is the same receptor that tastes Wasabi and Horseradish. CS Gas essentially tricks your body into thinking it is made of Wasabi.

### The Milk Myth: You always see people pouring milk on their eyes. Chemically? It’s mostly a placebo. The fat in milk might help dissolve the powder a little better than water, but saline or plain water works just as well (and doesn't rot if you miss a spot).

## Pepper Spray (OC)
The Sauce: This is just pure Capsaicin—the oil from chili peppers—concentrated to an insane degree. It’s nature’s way of saying "Go Away," bottled up and pressurized.
